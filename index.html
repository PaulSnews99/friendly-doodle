Holly Willoughby in hilarious double blunder live on This Morning thanks to camera edits and tricky names

Holly Willoughby had a bit of a mare on today's This Morning but she powered on through with her trademark giggles.The ITV host host fell foul to and awkward camera edit before struggling to pronounce a Strictly star's name. Holly first got caught out as she was introducing Shayne Ward who is the latest famous face to host the shows holiday competition.The 38-year-old then attempted to point to a rather glitzy gold countdown clock in the corner of the screen which tells viewers when the competition will come to an end.

Pointing her finger to the left of the telly screen, she said: "You see that clock in the corner?"But the problem was, Holly couldn't quite find the clock - and her co-host Phillip Schofield wasn't going to let her get away with it and the smirks quickly appeared across both of their faces.
